Such Doge. Such Game. Such wow.

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four doges below. By clicking on a doge you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each doge is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the doge starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
